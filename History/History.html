HISTORY

1983

Vocational school kilo met 5 was established  under the Ministry of Labor and Social Welfare.

1984

The Skill Development Center was established under the Department of Labor and Social Welfare Vientiane Capital.

1998

In 1998, the above two centers were merged into the Vientiane Capital Skill Development Center(VSDC). Facilitated by Department of Labor and Social Welfare of Vientiane Capital, VSDC supports community development.
