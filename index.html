About Vientiane Skill Development Center
Since its establishment in 1998, the Vientiane Skill Development Center (VSDC) has actively collaborated with international organizations, including the Korea International Cooperation Agency (KOICA), the National Information Society Agency of Korea (NIA), and Guangzhou Panyu Polytechnic in China.
Our mission here at VSDC is to empower the younger generations through skill development and to enhance the capabilities of the existing workforce. We are committed to equipping individuals with the knowledge, skills, and competencies necessary to succeed in today’s evolving job market.
